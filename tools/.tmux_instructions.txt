** Commands **
  - To move between panes:
      Ctrl+a, arrow
  - To exit:
      Ctrl+a, ':', 'kill-session'
** UART interfaces **
  - /embsec/UART0: reset
  - /embsec/UART1: bootloader interface
  - /embsec/UART2: output
  
To re-launch a connection to interface x, type:
    miniterm /embsec/UARTx
